Weekly Call Setup — look for all

  • Price near or bouncing off support level
  • RSI rising from oversold (<35) or near midline
  • TSI crosses above signal line
  • EMA 7 above EMA 21 (short-term uptrend)
  • Price above EMA 21

Weekly Put Setup — look for all

  • Price near or rejecting from resistance level
  • RSI falling from overbought (>65) or weakening
  • TSI crosses below signal line
  • EMA 7 below EMA 21 (short-term downtrend)
  • Price below EMA 21
